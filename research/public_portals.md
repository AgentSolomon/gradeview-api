## Key Findings

### Schools WITH Confirmed Public Grade Distribution Portals (9 confirmed)

1. **New York University (NYU)** - Law School publishes grade distributions as PDFs
   - URL: https://www.law.nyu.edu/sites/default/files/Spring%202021%20Grade%20Distribution.pdf

2. **University of Minnesota – Twin Cities** - Two sources for grade data
   - **Gopher Grades** (umn.lol) - Maintained by Social Coding, updated through Fall 2025
   - **Official** (idr.umn.edu) - University's Institutional Data and Research department

3. **Kennesaw State University** - Institutional Research dashboard
   - Direct URL: https://ir.kennesaw.edu/dashboards/grade-distribution.php
   - Alternative: https://campus.kennesaw.edu/offices-services/data-strategy/institutional-research/dashboards/grade-distribution.php

4. **Utah Valley University** - Institutional research support
   - URL: https://www.uvu.edu/birs/performance-indicators/academic-programs/grade_distribution.html

5. **UC San Diego (UCSD)** - Crowd-sourced project
   - **SunSET** - https://sheeptester.github.io/ucsd-sunset/
   - Data pulled from institutional records; maintained by student volunteers

6. **Cal State Fullerton** - Student-created grade distribution tool
   - **FullertonGrades** - Tracks 8,000+ classes with instructor information

7. **Virginia Tech** - University DataCommons system
   - URL: https://udc.vt.edu/irdata/data/courses/grades
   - Also: Collegiate Times maintains historical grades database

8. **University of Delaware** - Institutional Research and Effectiveness
   - URL: https://ire.udel.edu/ir/grade-distributions/
   - Faculty Senate voted in 2005 to make grade distributions public

9. **CUNY (City University of New York)**
   - **Brooklyn College**: https://www.brooklyn.cuny.edu/bc/offices/avpbandp/ipra/grade_distribution.htm
   - **City College**: https://www.ccny.cuny.edu/institutionalresearch/cuny-public-dashboards
   - Other CUNY schools (York College, etc.) also have grade distribution dashboards

## Research Methodology

For each school, searched using terms including:
- "{School Name} grade distribution site"
- "{School Name} institutional research grade distribution public"
- "{School Name} grade distribution database"
- "{School Name} course grades public portal"

Verified findings by checking official institutional research departments, registrar pages, and data dashboards.

## Notes for GradeView Development

1. **Official University Portals (Highest Value)**
   - Kennesaw State, Virginia Tech, Utah Valley, UC San Diego, University of Delaware: Official university data
   - Most accessible and legally compliant

2. **Student-Created Tools (Medium Value)**
   - FullertonGrades, Gopher Grades, SunSET: Crowdsourced or student-maintained
   - May have data quality/completeness concerns; verify legality of use

3. **Law School Data**
   - NYU Law publishes distributions; check if other law schools have similar public data

4. **CUNY System**
   - Multiple CUNY colleges have grade dashboards; check all 25 CUNY schools for data availability

5. **Potential Outreach Candidates (NO portal, but large enrollment)**
   - Arizona State University (125,000+), Liberty University (135,000+), Penn State (73,250)
   - These schools have no public portals but large enrollments; worth contacting for potential data partnerships
