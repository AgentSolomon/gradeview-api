Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

GradeView ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Do Not Collect

GradeView does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or log in
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or third-party trackers
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties

Grade Data

All grade distribution data displayed in GradeView is sourced from public records obtained through official public information requests submitted to universities under applicable open records laws. This data is aggregated and anonymized — no individual student records are displayed or stored.

App Usage

GradeView may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (such as which schools or courses are viewed most often) solely for the purpose of improving the app experience. This data cannot be used to identify any individual user.

Data Storage

All data displayed in the app is stored locally on your device or fetched from our servers in read-only form. We do not store any user-specific data on our servers.

Children's Privacy

GradeView is intended for use by college students and does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at admin@gradeview.app.
