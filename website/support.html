❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the grade data come from?

Grade distribution data is obtained through public records requests submitted to universities under applicable state open records laws. Data is provided as-is from each institution.

Why don't I see my school?

We're actively adding new universities. If your school isn't listed yet, email us at admin@gradeview.app and we'll prioritize it.

The app shows no data for a course — why?

Some courses may not have data available for the selected filters. Try adjusting the year or semester filter, or check if the department code is correct.

How current is the data?

Data coverage varies by school. Most schools provide data for the last 3–5 academic years. Coverage dates are shown within the app for each school.

I found an error in the data — how do I report it?

Please email admin@gradeview.app with the school, course, and what you believe is incorrect. We'll investigate and correct it as needed.

How do I request a feature?

We'd love to hear your ideas! Send feature requests to admin@gradeview.app.
