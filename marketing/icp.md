# GradeView — ICP.md
## Ideal Customer Profiles

---

## ICP 1: The GPA Protector
**Who:** Sophomore or junior, pre-med, pre-law, engineering, business
**Age:** 19-22
**Core fear:** One bad grade ruining their GPA and tanking their med school/law school/job chances
**Behavior:** Researches everything before committing. Reads every Rate My Professor review. Asks upperclassmen for advice. Spends hours on Reddit trying to find which professor is easier.
**What they want:** Certainty. Data. To know they made the right call before it's too late.
**Hook that works:** The GPA risk angle. "I can't afford a C in this class" energy.
**Example:** Pre-med at TAMU trying to protect their 3.8 GPA picking between 2 orgo professors

---

## ICP 2: The Smart Slacker
**Who:** Any major, any year — but values their free time as much as their grades
**Age:** 18-23
**Core fear:** Getting stuck in a class that destroys their semester for no reason
**Behavior:** Actively looks for the path of least resistance. Not lazy — strategic. Wants good grades AND a life.
**What they want:** To pass with the best grade possible while doing reasonable work
**Hook that works:** "Work smarter not harder" angle. The life hack framing.
**Example:** Junior who wants an A in their gen ed requirement without sacrificing their weekends

---

## ICP 3: The Overwhelmed Freshman
**Who:** First semester or first year college student
**Age:** 17-19
**Core fear:** Not knowing how college works. Making a mistake they can't undo.
**Behavior:** Asks older students for advice constantly. Overwhelmed by options. Doesn't know Rate My Professor exists yet.
**What they want:** Someone to just tell them what to do
**Hook that works:** "I wish someone told me this freshman year" angle. Older student sharing wisdom.
**Example:** Freshman at UT Austin about to register for their first semester, completely lost

---

## ICP 4: The Registration Rush Planner
**Who:** Any student, any year — registration window just opened or is coming up
**Age:** 18-24
**Core fear:** Missing out on the good sections, getting stuck with a bad professor
**Behavior:** Extremely active on Reddit, Discord, GroupMe during registration windows. Refreshes Rate My Professor. Asks friends frantically.
**What they want:** Fast answers before all the good spots fill up
**Hook that works:** Urgency + speed. "Registration opens in 2 days" energy.
**Example:** Any student whose registration window is this week

---

## ICP 5: The Transfer Student / New School
**Who:** Transfer student or student at a new campus
**Age:** 19-24
**Core fear:** Not having the inside knowledge that continuing students have. Starting at a disadvantage.
**Behavior:** Desperately trying to get intel on professors and classes they know nothing about
**What they want:** The same information advantage that students who've been there 2 years have
**Hook that works:** The outsider who found the cheat code angle.

---

## Universal truths across all ICPs
- They all have registered for a class and regretted it
- They all know someone who got wrecked by a hard grader
- They all want an A — or at minimum, to not get surprised
- They respond to peer-to-peer advice, not marketing
- TikTok and Instagram are where they discover apps
- They forward useful things to their friends immediately
