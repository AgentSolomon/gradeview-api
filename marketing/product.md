# GradeView — Product.md
## What GradeView Actually Is

### One sentence
GradeView shows you the real grade distributions for courses and professors at your university — straight from public university records.

### What it does
- Shows exactly how many students got A, B, C, D, F in any course
- Breaks it down by professor
- Lets you compare professors teaching the same course side by side
- Shows data across multiple semesters so you can spot trends
- Covers multiple universities (TAMU, UT Austin, UW-Madison — more coming)
- Free iOS app

### What makes it different from Rate My Professor
- Rate My Professor = opinions. Subjective. Can be faked. Biased.
- GradeView = actual grade data from the university itself. Numbers don't lie.
- You can see: Professor A gives 80% A's. Professor B gives 30% A's. Same class. Pick accordingly.

### The core insight we sell
There are easy professors and hard professors teaching the exact same course.
Most students don't know this until it's too late.
GradeView shows you before you register.

### Real use cases
- Picking between two professors for the same class
- Figuring out if a class will tank your GPA before you take it
- Finding which semester a course is graded more leniently
- Helping a friend who's struggling by showing them a professor with better grade outcomes

### Data facts
- Data is sourced from public university records (TPIA requests, public datasets)
- We display it as-is — we don't modify or editorialize
- Updated as new data becomes available
- Currently: TAMU (102K+ records), UT Austin, UW-Madison
