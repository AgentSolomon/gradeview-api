## NOTES FOR FILMING
- Film vertically, phone quality is fine — looks more authentic
- Scripts 01, 03, 05, 06, 07, 08, 09 = talking head (just you on camera)
- Scripts 02, 04, 10 = screen recording of the app works great
- Best performers to test first: 03 (freshman hook), 04 (side by side demo), 09 (tag a friend)
- Keep it under 30 seconds — watch time is everything
- Don't add music — let the words land
